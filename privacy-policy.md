# Privacy Policy

*Last updated: July 2026*

## What this site collects

This site does not run analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts.
No personal data is collected by thetokenguy.net directly.

## Infrastructure

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages and served through Cloudflare. Both
services may log standard web request data (IP addresses, browser type,
pages visited) as part of normal infrastructure operation. Their respective
privacy policies apply:

- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
- [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/)

## Cookies

This site does not set cookies. Cloudflare may set a technical cookie
(__cf_bm) for bot protection purposes. No tracking or advertising cookies
are used.

## External links

Posts on this site link to external resources including Microsoft
documentation, GitHub repositories, and third-party tools. This policy
does not cover those sites.
